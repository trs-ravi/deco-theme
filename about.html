About Decor Listings

We are a website which curates the best of Home Décor in India. We have carefully selected only those products that are aesthetic and can enhance the beauty of your space. We have carefully selected products, tagged them in the appropriate category so that its easy for you to search for them. We are affiliate partners to reputed companies like Amazon.

With our website you can:

  • Look up for Home Decor products in the various categories
  • Search for specific dealers for a product or a company
  • Shortlist the products you may like
  • You can enquire or buy from our affiliate partners
  • Follow us on Instagram or Facebook for daily inspiration on Home Decor

We hope that our website would help you in doing up your home or office.
